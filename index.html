Travel resources and recommendations

Welcome to London!

This is the second most visited city in the world in 2018, with a bustling nightlife, fascinating history and lots of opportunities to shop!

Make sure you plan your trip as events sell out in advance, and prices are generally high!

visitlondon.com
#1 Most Visited: Big Ben

Big Ben is the nickname for the Great Bell of the clock at the north end of the Palace of Westminster in London and is usually extended to refer to both the clock and the clock tower.

#2 Most Visited: London Eye

The London eye is a giant Ferris Wheel (tallest in Europe) on the South Bank of the River Thames in London.

#3 Most Visited: Buckingham Palace

Buckingham Palace is the London residence and administrative headquarters of the monarch of the United Kingdom. Located in the City of Westminster, the palace is often at the centre of state occasions and royal hospitality.
